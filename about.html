The Legacy of Atreyapuram

Nestled on the banks of the Godavari river, Atreyapuram is a village synonymous with the paper-sweet distinct to Andhra Pradesh—Putharekulu.

For over three centuries, the women of Atreyapuram have mastered the delicate art of making these paper-thin rice starch sheets. It is a craft passed down through generations, requiring immense skill, patience, and the perfect pot temperature.

In 2021, Atreyapuram Putharekulu received the prestigious GI (Geographical Indication) Tag, recognizing its unique cultural and historical significance.

Our Mission
